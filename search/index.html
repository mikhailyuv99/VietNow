Frequently asked questions

Do I need a Telegram account to use this search?

No. You can search and read everything from the website. Only if you want to reply to a post or join the conversation do you need Telegram — clicking "Open in Telegram" on any result will take you there.

Is this available in English and Vietnamese?

Yes. Most messages are written in Russian by the community, but each message is automatically translated into English and Vietnamese. Switch the language with the RU / EN / VI toggle above the results.

How fresh are the results?

New messages usually appear within a minute. You can filter by the last hour, 24 hours, week, month, or year to focus on what's most relevant to you.

How does search work under the hood?

A small service reads the public community channel, tags each message by topic and city, and translates it. The website then searches those messages — just like a mini Google over the chat.

Can I add my business or remove a message?

Yes. Use the chat bubble in the bottom right corner, or email us through the Contact link in the footer. We review every request manually.
