Frequently asked questions

Do I need a Telegram account to use this search?

No. You can search and read everything from the website. Only if you want to reply to a post or join the conversation do you need Telegram — clicking "Open in Telegram" on any result will take you there.

Is this available in English and Vietnamese?

Yes. Most messages are written in Russian by the community, but each message is automatically translated into English and Vietnamese. Switch the language in the Filters panel.

How fresh are the results?

New messages usually appear within a minute. Use Filters → Time range to focus on the last hour, day, week, month, or year.

How does search work under the hood?

A small service reads the public community chat, tags each message by topic and city, and translates it. The website then searches those messages.

Can I add my business or remove a message?

Yes. Use the chat bubble in the bottom right corner, or the Contact link in the footer. We review every request manually.
